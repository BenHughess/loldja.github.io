My name is Lauren, and I am a Data Scientist.

Most recently, I've was an Associate at BFA, a niche financial inclusion global advisory firm, on the Quantitative Analytics & Inclusive Fintech team. There I developed train/test/validation protocols for predictive API products as part of the Catalyst Fund and implemented BI dashboards in Looker for pay-as-you-go solar panels. I also wrote and won a $100,000 contract with UNCDF.

Previously, I focused on the monitoring and evaluation (M&E) of international health programs. I am an experienced manager and technical advisor, having overseen program and research field teams in South Sudan, Bangladesh, and South Africa. My scientific publications are on PubMed.

I am a Contributor at Towards Data Science and a Python mentor at codebar.

MIT and Hopkins trained in quantitative and qualitative methods.

I am also an alumna of the Metis Data Science bootcamp.

My family owns a popular kitchen remodeling business.

Proud member of EFF.

Conversant in Spanish (advanced) and French (beginner).
